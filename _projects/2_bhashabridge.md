BhashaBridge was built to handle the gap between literal translation and actual cultural understanding in mixed-language chats. It uses Gemini to interpret tone, slang, and conversational context across Hinglish, Kanglish, Tanglish, and similar language blends, then surfaces private inline explanations and tone-matched reply suggestions inside Telegram.
