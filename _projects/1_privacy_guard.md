Built as a hackathon project, Unified Privacy Guard monitors ambient viewers and on-screen content to protect sensitive information in real time. The system ties together a browser extension, face-counting service, FastAPI endpoints, and a controller that dims the display whenever multiple people are present and the current page is classified as sensitive.
